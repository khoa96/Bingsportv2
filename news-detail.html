Inter Milan 3-0 Spezia

Romelu Lukaku was the highlight of Inter Milan's 3-0 victory over Spezia on Saturday, their second victory in as many Serie A games after his return from Chelsea.

In the 82nd minute, Dzeko assisted fellow replacement Joaquin Correa in scoring the third goal.

Before halftime, Lukaku nearly added to his goal from Inter's opening victory over recently promoted Lecce by crashing a header off the crossbar.

Although Inter enjoyed an early advantage, the home crowd had to wait until the 35th minute for Martinez to score. Lukaku returned a cross from Nicolo Barella for the Argentine to shoot inside the left post.

In the 52nd minute, Milan Skriniar received a pass from Lukaku after the Belgian sped past one defender and faced five further opponents. Despite being stopped, Skriniar's attempted return pass dropped nicely for Alhanolu to score.

Also on Saturday, Sassuolo defeated Lecce 1-0 thanks to a fantastic strike from Domenico Berardi in the first half.
